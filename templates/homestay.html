About Holasay Homestay

Set in the apple-orchard village of Kalpa, just above Reckong Peo, Holasay Homestay offers a genuine taste of Kinnauri hospitality. Wake up to views of the Kinner Kailash range, enjoy home-cooked local food, and let our hosts help you plan your days around Kalpa, Kinnaur and the Sangla valley.

It's the perfect base whether you're here for the apple orchards, the monasteries, or simply to relax in one of the most scenic corners of Himachal Pradesh.
