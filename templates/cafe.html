About The Bait Cafe

Located right in Reckong Peo, the gateway town to Kalpa in Kinnaur, The Bait Cafe has become a favourite stop for locals and travellers searching for the best food in Kalpa region. From freshly brewed coffee to warm Himalayan bakery treats and comforting local dishes, every plate here is made with care.

Whether you're heading up to Kalpa, exploring the Sangla valley, or simply passing through Reckong Peo, The Bait Cafe offers a cosy place to relax with a view of the Kinner Kailash range.
