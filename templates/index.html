Everything About The Bait Cafe

Reckong Peo's favourite spot for the best local food in Kalpa & Kinnaur

About Us

A Warm Corner of Reckong Peo

The Bait Cafe sits right in the heart of Reckong Peo, the gateway town to Kalpa in Kinnaur. What started as a small bakery counter has grown into one of the most loved cafes in the region — known for serving the best food in Kalpa with a smile.

Every dish is prepared fresh, every cup of coffee hand-brewed, and every table comes with a view of the Himalayas. Whether you're a local or just passing through on your way up to Kalpa, Sangla, or Chitkul, The Bait Cafe is the perfect stop.

🏡 HOLASAY HOMESTAY

Everything About Holasay Homestay

A cosy, family-run homestay near Reckong Peo with views of Kinner Kailash

About Us

Home Away From Home Near Kalpa

Holasay Homestay is set in the orchard village of Telangi, just near Reckong Peo. Run by a local Kinnauri family, it offers genuine hospitality, comfortable rooms, and home-cooked meals made with ingredients grown right in the surrounding orchards and fields.

Guests often say it feels less like a hotel and more like staying with family — with the added bonus of one of the best mountain views in all of Kinnaur.
